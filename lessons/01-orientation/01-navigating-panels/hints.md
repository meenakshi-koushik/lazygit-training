## Hint 1

Use `<tab>` to cycle through each panel and watch the preview pane on the right update. Try pressing `1` through `5` to jump directly to each panel. Spend a moment scrolling up and down (`j`/`k`) in the Commits panel to see the history.

## Hint 2

To create a new branch, navigate to the **Branches** panel (press `3` or `<tab>` until you reach it). With any branch selected, press `n` to create a new branch. Type `explore-panels` as the name and press `<enter>`.

## Hint 3

To stage a file, go to the **Files** panel (press `2`). Use `j`/`k` to highlight a file, then press `<space>` to stage it. The file's icon will change to show it's staged. Once you have at least one file staged, press `c` to open the commit message editor, type a message containing the word "explore" (e.g., `explore: testing lazygit panels`), and press `<enter>` to commit.
